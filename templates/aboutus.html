About Us

TumorDetect is a web application designed to help users detect and monitor brain tumors using advanced AI algorithms. Our mission is to provide accurate, easy-to-use, and accessible medical support tools for everyone.

The system combines image preprocessing, data visualization, transfer learning, model building, and Flask deployment to make tumor detection accessible online. Users can upload MRI images and receive AI-generated results quickly and easily.
