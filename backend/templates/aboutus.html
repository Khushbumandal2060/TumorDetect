About Us

This Tumor Detection System is a web application designed to help users detect and monitor brain tumors using advanced AI algorithms. Our mission is to provide accurate, easy-to-use, and accessible medical support tools for everyone.

This system combines image preprocessing, data visualization, transfer learning, model building, and Flask deployment to make tumor detection accessible online. Users can upload MRI images and get clear, AI-generated results.
